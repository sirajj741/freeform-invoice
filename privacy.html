Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

FreeForm.io does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All invoice data you enter stays on your device and is never sent to any server.

When you save a draft, your invoice data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your computer. You can delete it anytime by clearing your browser data.
